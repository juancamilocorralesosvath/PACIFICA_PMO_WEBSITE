COMMUNITY INVOLVEMENT

Community support initiatives are ongoing across all the projects where we have operations. Through each and every one of our facilities, we are building trust and credibility with local and international stakeholders. We recognize that our performance has to be demonstrable. In an effort to ensure common interpretation and implementation of our environmental policies where we operate, we have committed to jointly fund community initiatives that focus on areas like:

Conservation and preservation
Education and research
Restoration of any impact from our operations
Economic development of communities where we operate
